Our Process

From Concept to Construction

Stage 01

Feasibility & Concept

We begin by assessing the potential of your site. We explore design options that maximize value and space while navigating local planning constraints. This is where your vision starts to take shape.

  • Site Appraisal
  • Concept Sketches
  • Pre-application Advice
  • Measured Surveys
Stage 02

Planning Applications

We handle the complex bureaucracy of the planning system. We prepare detailed architectural drawings and design and access statements to give your project the best chance of approval.

  • Full Planning Permission
  • Householder Applications
  • Listed Building Consent
  • Heritage Statements
Stage 03

Technical Design

Once planning is approved, we add the technical detail required for construction. We produce Building Regulations drawings and coordinate with structural engineers to ensure the build is safe and compliant.

  • Building Regulations
  • Construction Details
  • Structural Coordination
  • Specification
